Prompt
It is the input or instruction given to an AI model to generate a response.

Prompt Engg
it is the process of crafting and refining prompts to improve the peerformance of ai models.

Key Parts of Prompt
1. Directive
It is a concise instruction to AI model to perform a task.
2. Examples
3. Role (persona)
4. output formatting
5. additional information (context/knowledge base)

There is no order in above parts.
But what order matters is like:
    1. <context+examples+knowledge base>
    2. <directive>
    3. <output format>
This matters as the flow of information processing matters.
